Pretty-print dates

  • April 1, 2021
  • Posted by John Doe
  • How to, Technology

To write out an ISO 8601 timestamp in the current language, you can use the date_i18n shortcode:

Writing

{{% date_i18n "2020-10-20" %}}

will result in

October 20, 2020
